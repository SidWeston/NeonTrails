Applying Textures

First Import the folder of the asset you want to add

Then right click and press unpack model completely

In that folder you imported right click, go to create and then material

Then click the new made material

Then drag the textures that you imported to the different maps 

BaseColour/ Abedo map goes in the Abedo map section

Metallic goes in metalic

Normal goes in normal but you may have to press fix now when imported

Height goes in Height

Then Roughness goes in details
